# Support

In case of problems, as well as for questions and suggestions, there are two main
support channels. Before asking, you may also want to check the
[Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md) page — your issue may already have a known
solution.

## CERN Mattermost channel

Join the
[Purdue AF support channel on CERN Mattermost](https://mattermost.web.cern.ch/cms-exp/channels/purdue-analysis-facility)
(CERN login required). This is the fastest way to reach both the admins and other
users.

## Purdue AF mailing list

Send a message to the following address:
[cms-af@groups.purdue.edu](mailto:cms-af@groups.purdue.edu).

This mailing list is also used for important announcements and alerts about new
features.

### Subscribing to the mailing list

Users with a Purdue email can subscribe by managing their group membership at the
[Microsoft Exchange admin center](https://admin.exchange.microsoft.com/#/):

1. Log in with your Purdue career account.
2. Click "groups" in the sidebar.
3. Under the section "Groups I belong to", click on the "Join..." icon.
4. Search for `cms-af`.
5. Select "Purdue CMS Analysis Facility" and click "Join group".

Unsubscribing from the mailing list is managed via the same portal.

## What to include in a support request

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

* your AF username and the login method you used (Purdue / CERN / FNAL);
* the approximate time when the problem occurred;
* what you were trying to do, and the full text of any error messages.
